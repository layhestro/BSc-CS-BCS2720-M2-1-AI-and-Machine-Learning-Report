\subsection{RAM Usage Prediction Results (RQ3)}
\label{sec:rq3_results}

We evaluated six different regression models to predict peak RAM usage for both PPO and SAC algorithms. The performance of each model was measured using the Coefficient of Determination ($R^2$) and Mean Absolute Error (MAE). Table~\ref{tab:ram_results} summarizes these results.

    \centering
    \caption{Performance comparison of RAM usage predictors for PPO and SAC algorithms.}
    \label{tab:ram_results}

        & \multicolumn{2}{c|}{\textbf{PPO}} & \multicolumn{2}{c}{\textbf{SAC}} \\
        \textbf{Model} & \textbf{R² Score} & \textbf{MAE (MB)} & \textbf{R² Score} & \textbf{MAE (MB)} \\
        \hline
        KNN Regressor & 0.99 & 57.54 & 0.94 & 633.39 \\
        Linear Regression & 0.83 & 1552.14 & 0.86 & 1797.45 \\
        Random Forest & 0.99 & 60.20 & 0.96 & 620.07 \\
        Extra Trees & 0.99 & 81.67 & 0.96 & 621.97 \\
        Hist. Gradient Boosting & 0.99 & 236.61 & 0.96 & 613.18 \\
        ANN (MLP Regressor) & -8.82 & 1852.35 & 0.52 & 3747.60 \\
        \hline
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}

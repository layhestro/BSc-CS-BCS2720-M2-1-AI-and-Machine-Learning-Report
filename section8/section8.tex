\section{Conclusion}

\subsection{Summary}
We propose a three-phase plan to predict properties of Unity ML-Agents training runs using supervised ML. The project couples a tiered experimental design (15 environments; PPO/SAC/POCA/IL) with automated logging and a reproducible repo. Roles, risks, and milestones are defined to ensure timely delivery and data quality.
